:::note Sandbox expiration
The sandbox is free for 14 days. After 14 days, the sandbox expires. All data is deleted when a sandbox expires.

To retrieve a copy of your sandbox data before it is deleted, use the [cursor API](/developers/weaviate/api/rest/objects#exhaustive-listing-using-a-cursor-after).

To preserve your data and upgrade to a paid instance, [contact us](mailto:support@weaviate.io) for help.
:::
